Installation
============

Download and extract the following files from
"http://rpg.ifi.uzh.ch/datasets/sin2_tex2_h1_v8_d.tar.gz" to "/Draft/SLAM/datasets/SVO/sin2_tex2_h1_v8_d/":
- the "sin2_tex2_h1_v8_d/img" directory
- the "sin2_tex2_h1_v8_d/trajectory_nominal.txt" file, but rename it to "traj_groundtruth.txt"
